5 Core Reading Strategies

Master the exact tactical workflows required for each VSTEP question category.

📌

1. Main Idea & Subject (Ý chính)

  • Read the first 1-2 lines of each paragraph.
  • Identify the recurring topic noun across the entire text.
  • Check if the option covers the WHOLE text, not just one detail.
⚠️ Common Distractor Trap
Too Narrow: Picking a specific example from one paragraph as the main idea of the passage.
🔤

2. Vocabulary & Pronoun Reference

  • Locate target word and read +/- 1 surrounding sentence.
  • Substitute candidate words to check context alignment.
  • For pronouns (it/they/those), trace back to singular/plural antecedents.
⚠️ Common Distractor Trap
Literal Dictionary Fallacy: Choosing a word's common meaning when the context demands an academic secondary meaning.
🔎

3. Factual Details & EXCEPT Questions

  • Highlight key nouns, dates, or terms in the question stem.
  • Scan the passage rapidly to locate the exact paragraph.
  • For EXCEPT questions: 3 options are stated; 1 is false/unmentioned.
⚠️ Common Distractor Trap
Subtle Misstatements: Options using text words but reversing relationships (e.g., 'softer mineral scratches harder one').
💡

4. Inferences & Author's Purpose

  • Inferences must be 100% logically proven by text facts.
  • Avoid speculative guesses outside the passage boundaries.
  • Author's Purpose: Ask 'Why is this example included here?'
⚠️ Common Distractor Trap
Over-assumption: Selecting plausible real-world statements that are NOT supported by passage facts.
🧩

5. Sentence Insertion & Paraphrasing

  • Check transition signals (However, Thus, For instance).
  • Look for reference anchors (This process, These maps).
  • Paraphrase: Retain core subject + predicate + key condition.
⚠️ Common Distractor Trap
Omission Trap: Paraphrase options that miss crucial qualifying conditions (e.g. 'only in winter').
